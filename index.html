Kampala

Kampala is the capital city of Uganda. It serves as a market center and the most highly populated city in Uganda. The city is a center of Uganda's economy with most of commercial centers, recreation centers, banks, public firms and its where most of the international agencies headquarters are staged. It is also the largest city in Uganda.
